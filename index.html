what is this site about :

This site lists all the ski resorts in France with detailed information such as snow conditions, ski pass prices, available pistes and activities offered. We created this site to help skiers and snowboarders plan their trip to the mountains with peace of mind and with up-to-date information. We are passionate about winter sports and we take pride in sharing our knowledge and experience with our visitors. Our mission is to make planning your ski trip to France easy, by providing you with a user-friendly, informative and convenient platform. We hope you enjoy our site and find all the information you need to plan your next mountain adventure.
